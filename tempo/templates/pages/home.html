Welcome to Tempo

Tempo is your personal log, a place where you can easily store events that happens to you.

Wether you're on a new diet and want to keep a track of how much calories you get, want to quit smoking or track how much you spend, Tempo can help you gather important data and analyze it.

Get started

Gather

Send your events to Tempo and browse them using the timeline or our built-in search and tagging functions.

This is really easy, and accessible from any device, from your smartphone to your desktop computer.

Analyze

Better understand what happens in your life: Ever wanted to know how much cigarettes you smoke each week? How much time you spend in transports? How often you are sick?

Tempo makes it possible to track such events and run queries against the data.

Share

Give access to your events and statistics to anybody thanks to our powerful sharing features.

Open Source and Free

Tempo is fully open-source and free, as in free speech and free beer!

This means you can hack it, host it on your own infrastructure and protect. You don't have to trust anybody else for the safety and the privacy of your data.

Integrations

Easily gather data from other sources and applications or send events to third-party services.
